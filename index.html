Investor Philosophies
🎩 Warren Buffett
Seeks companies with durable competitive advantages (moats), consistent high ROE (>15%), low debt, and strong free cash flow. Buys at fair prices and holds forever.

Key metrics: ROE >15% · FCF >10% · P/E <30 · D/E <2
🧠 Charlie Munger
Focuses on quality businesses with strong moats and pricing power. Willing to pay up for truly exceptional businesses.

Key metrics: ROE >20% · FCF >15% · Clear moat
📈 Peter Lynch
Famous for "invest in what you know". Looks for undervalued growth stocks with P/E below 20.

Key metrics: P/E <20 · ROE >10% · PEG <1
📐 Benjamin Graham
The "father of value investing". Very strict: P/E under 15, dividend yield, and strong balance sheet.

Key metrics: P/E <15 · Dividend >2% · D/E <1
💰 Dividend Income
Focus on companies with strong, growing dividends. Ideal for passive income.

Key metrics: Yield >2.5% · ROE >10%
🚀 High Growth
Targets companies with exceptional ROE and strong free cash flow. Often technology and platform businesses.

Key metrics: ROE >25% · FCF >20%
